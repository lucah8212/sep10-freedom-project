About

Coming up with the idea for the PilotVision Glasses came from research done on the technology that we have today in aviation, from software that assists pilots, to as complex as collision-avoidance systems.

Things that Exist today

Software

  • Garmin Pilot
  • ForeFlight

    • Real-time charts are even more helpful for pilots during departure and arrival stages of the flights

  • Station Weather

All three apps serve the main purpose of navigation for pilots, along with weather briefings and a variety of charts

Hardware

  • Flight Computer

    • Performs critical aviation computations, including time, speed, distance, heading, wind, fuel, altitude, and more. Integrated calculator which solves multiple flight-related problems in a single function.
